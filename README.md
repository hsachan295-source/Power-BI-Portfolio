# Power BI Portfolio: Data Modeling & Business Intelligence Dashboards

Welcome to my Power BI Portfolio! This repository contains a curated collection of Business Intelligence dashboards, reports, and case studies developed during my data science training.

This portfolio highlights my skills in **End-to-End Analytics**, including ETL (Power Query), Data Modeling (Star Schema), Advanced DAX, and Interactive UI/UX Design.

---

## 🚀 Key Skills Demonstrated
* **ETL & Data Preparation:** Cleaning, transforming, and merging datasets using Power Query.
* **Data Modeling:** Designing relationships (1-to-many, active vs. inactive), Star Schemas, and optimizing data schemas for efficient reporting.
* **Calculated Metrics (DAX):** Writing custom measures, calculated columns, time-intelligence calculations, and context transition modifiers.
* **Interactive Visualization:** Creating intuitive dashboards with custom tooltips, conditional formatting, bookmarks, parameters, drill-throughs, and dynamic slicers.

## 📊 Project Showcases

> [!TIP]
> **To Recruiters:** You can click the links below to view the PDF versions of the reports directly on GitHub, or download the `.pbix` files to open them in Power BI Desktop.

### 🔹 Project 1: Sales Performance Dashboard
* **Objective:** Analyze monthly sales trends, profitability margins, and regional performance to identify underperforming sectors.
* **Key Visuals & Features:**
  * Interactive KPI cards tracking total revenue, sales growth, and profit margin.
  * Trend lines using DAX Time Intelligence (YTD, YoY Growth).
  * Top-performing products and sales representative rankings.
* **Tech Stack used:** Power Query, Star Schema Modeling, DAX, Custom KPI visuals.
* **Quick View:** 📂 [Download PBIX](./Report%20And%20Design%20Practices-1.pbix)

### 🔹 Project 2: Customer Churn & Retention Analytics
* **Objective:** Visualize customer demographics, churn rate, and lifetime value to support marketing retention campaigns.
* **Key Visuals & Features:**
  * Churn distribution maps and customer cohort analysis.
  * Dynamic filters for age group, tenure, and subscription type.
  * Advanced DAX formulas calculating churn percentage and moving averages.
* **Tech Stack used:** Advanced DAX, Bookmark navigator for swapping views, Parameter slicers.
* **Quick View:** 📂 [Download PBIX](./Phase%204%20completed.pbix)

### 🔹 Project 3: Data Modeling & Relationships Schema
* **Objective:** Design a clean Star Schema data model, resolving inactive relationships and cross-filtering issues.
* **Key Visuals & Features:**
  * Clean Star Schema with clearly mapped facts and dimensions.
  * Handling active vs. inactive relationships using DAX functions (like `USERELATIONSHIP`).
  * Modeling cross-filter directions to optimize filter propagation across tables.
* **Tech Stack used:** Data Modeling, Star Schema, Relationships, Active/Inactive relationship DAX.
* **Quick View:** 📂 [Download PBIX](./Phase%203%20data%20modeling%20completed.pbix)
